About

A global hub within the campus of OP Jindal Global University, JSiE aims to inspire the best and the brightest students to be entrepreneurs to creatively address the world’s most pressing challenges. Designed to enhance collaboration, JSiE provides budding entrepreneurs with mentorship, resources, access to capital, publicity and connections to top talent. By engaging in interdisciplinary, problem-centric research alongside industry and community partners, JSiE aims to contribute to global sustainable development and the transformation of North India into a more inclusive society.
